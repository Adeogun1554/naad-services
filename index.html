Who We Are

NAAD began as a community-focused provider of non-emergency transportation services, built on the values of service, reliability, and compassion. Today, we're growing into something more — expanding into technology, workforce development, and solutions that meet real-world needs.

We are a noble organization guided by the belief that meaningful change starts with action. Whether it’s getting people to their appointments or helping them access digital tools for a better future, our mission remains the same: to bridge gaps, open doors, and uplift lives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities by delivering reliable services and innovative solutions — from accessible transportation to emerging technologies — that improve lives and create lasting impact.

What We Do

Non-Emergency Transportation

Safe, dependable rides for individuals who need access to medical appointments and essential services.

Tech Literacy & Access

Workshops and training programs to empower individuals with digital skills for today's workforce.

Youth & Workforce Development

Equipping the next generation with tools, mentorship, and pathways to long-term success.

Community Partnerships

Collaborating with local leaders and organizations to expand reach and deepen impact.
